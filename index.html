About Us

Welcome to Noire and Blanche, where black meets white in perfect harmony. Nestled in the heart of Lekki Phase I, we've created an intimate sanctuary for coffee lovers, creatives, and those seeking a moment of refined tranquility.

Our philosophy is simple: exceptional coffee, thoughtfully designed spaces, and genuine hospitality. Every detail—from our bamboo-slat ceiling to our marble counter—has been carefully curated to create an experience that feels both welcoming and elevated.

Whether you're here for a morning espresso, a working session, or an evening with friends, Noire and Blanche is your space to slow down and savor the moment.
